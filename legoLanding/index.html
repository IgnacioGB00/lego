Temple of Tranquility

Discover the perfect blend of tradition and creativity — build your own Japanese Temple with LEGO and let ancient Japan inspire your imagination.

Build a peaceful sanctuary in the sky with Temple of Tranquility. This floating island set features a multi-tiered Japanese pagoda, serene gardens, stone lanterns, and lush forests. With detailed architectural elements and vibrant foliage, it captures the essence of traditional Japanese design in a magical LEGO format. Perfect for display or storytelling, this set invites builders to explore the harmony between nature and culture—brick by brick

Modular

Traditional Japanese temple surrounded by shrines and nature. Perfect for display or as part of an East-Asian inspired collection.A fictional design inspired by classical Japanese architecture, incorporating elements from temples like Kiyomizu-dera and Kyoto shrines. Set atop a floating island, it blends realistic architecture with a touch of fantasy.

Main Temple

The spiritual heart of the model. This three-tier pagoda with curved roofs represents the harmony between heaven, earth, and spirit.

Vegetation

A lush floating forest that surrounds and protects the temple. Each tree is built using layered techniques for natural depth and variety.

Pieces: 95

Support Structure
