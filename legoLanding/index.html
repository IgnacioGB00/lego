Temple of Tranquility

Discover the perfect blend of tradition and creativity — build your own Japanese Temple with LEGO and let ancient Japan inspire your imagination.

Build a peaceful sanctuary in the sky with Temple of Tranquility. This floating island set features a multi-tiered Japanese pagoda, serene gardens, stone lanterns, and lush forests. Perfect for display or storytelling, this set invites builders to explore the harmony between nature and culture—brick by brick
